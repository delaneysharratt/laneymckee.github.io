Marquee (Mobile)

Made for avid movie/television watchers, Marquee makes recommendations based on what friends on the app are watching and allows members to keep track of everything they watch and plan to watch. Members can create a personal watch list where they can organize their queued-up shows by priority, as well as add already watched shows to a public profile that friends can view.
